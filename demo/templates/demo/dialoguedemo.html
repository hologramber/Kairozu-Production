This is an example of the Exercise interface for Dialogues; it is best viewed on a large screen. Each dialogue begins with a prompt -- in the case below, the green chat bubble from やまださん. Your response is given in English, which you will enter in Japanese. As responses are entered correctly, they're added to your orange chat bubbles. The first few dialogues feel, at best, like talking to a chatbot from the 90s. As more grammar is acquired the dialogues become more fluid and relevant to everyday life.
