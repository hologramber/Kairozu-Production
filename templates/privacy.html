Privacy Policy

I probably care about your privacy more than you do. I wish more people cared more about the privacy of their data.

It should be obvious that when you register for an account your progress when it comes to Kairozu content is tracked -- the site wouldn't work otherwise. I can't say "you need to review word X" if I don't keep track of how well you've recalled word X in the past. That said, let me be clear:

  • No information collected (access records, user progress, how many times you've forgotten a verb, email address, etc) will ever be sold, traded, or otherwise disclosed to a third party for marketing purposes.
  • I won't send you thinly veiled spam emails informing you of new and amazing opportunities.
  • I will attempt to protect such personal information against loss and/or theft to the best of my ability.

I do use Google Analytics with all of the data-sharing and data collection for marketing options disabled. I do NOT use the User-ID feature.
