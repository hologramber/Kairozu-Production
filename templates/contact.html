Contact

Please remember that this is not a professional endeavor. I have a normal job that is 100% unrelated to Kairozu. I built this as a fun side project and thus it changes form and function frequently. I don't guarantee that the site will exist tomorrow.

Before you send me a grammar-related comment/fix/request, don't forget that many of the sentences are primarily built to assist with practicing specific grammar constructs. They might sound awkward, or you might never use them in a normal conversation, but if they're grammatically correct they're okay. That said, I'm happy to receive grammar fixes because I'll undoubtedly learn something at the same time -- if you wouldn't mind explaining why you believe something is wrong, I'd appreciate it. Thanks in advance!

If you run into a technical error, I'd also love to hear about it. Please include your browser & OS version, and what you were doing when the error occurred.

If you're angry that I accidentally taught you to say that you like eating cats for breakfast, well, I tried to warn you.
